Our Mission

Welcome to Plant Talk—the go-to community for plant lovers of all kinds. Whether you’re a beginner nurturing your first succulent or a seasoned gardener with a rainforest of houseplants, we’re here to help you grow, share, and learn.

Key Features

  • User Accounts & Authentication: Sign up with your email and secure password. Sessions are managed via cookies with configurable expiration.
  • Posts & Comments: Registered members can start new discussions—share tips on watering schedules, propagation tricks, and more—and reply to others.
  • Categories & Filters: Browse by plant type (e.g., Succulents, Tropical, Edibles). Filter to see your own posts or posts you’ve liked.
  • Reactions: Like or dislike posts and comments. Reaction counts show community favorites at a glance.
  • Image Uploads: Share photos of your plant babies in JPEG, PNG, or GIF (up to 20 MB). Show off that new bloom!
  • Advanced Features: Real-time notifications when someone likes or comments on your posts, an activity dashboard, and full edit/delete controls.
  • Docker-Ready: Containerized for easy deployment—get your own Plant Talk instance up and running in minutes.

Technology Stack

Backend: Go (net/http), SQLite
Auth Containerization: Docker
Frontend: Plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JS (no frameworks)

Getting Started

To run a local copy of Plant Talk:

  1. Clone the repo:
    2. git clone https://github.com/yourorg/plant-talk.git
  2. Build the Docker image:
    3. docker build -t plant-talk .
  3. Run the container:
    4. docker run -p 8080:8080 plant-talk
  4. Visit https://localhost:8080 in your browser.
